Pee-wee’s Playhouse was an American children’s television show that ran from 1986 to 1991. One of the characters in the show, The King of Cartoons, would come into the playhouse with an old television and play a cartoon from the golden age of American animation.

I created a letterhead package for this character, with an original logo, envelope, and letterhead and business card with a pop-up television similar to the one used in the show.
