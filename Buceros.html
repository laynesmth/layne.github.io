Every year, the Audubon Zoo holds a black tie event known as the Whitney Zoo-To-Do. As an exploration of potential parting favors to be given to donors of the Audubon Zoo at this event, I designed a series of three chocolate based on the Rhinoceros Hornbill, an exotic bird housed at the zoo.
