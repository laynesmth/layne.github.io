Dr. A. Victor Coonin, a Professor of Art History at Rhodes College, was a guest lecturer at Mississippi State University. His lecture, title David: From Renaissance Icon to Modern Activist, focused on the censorship of The David statue over time, since its creation and even in modern times.

To promote the lecture, I created a poster that uses weaved paper to block out portions of the image of The David, illustrating that idea of censorship.
